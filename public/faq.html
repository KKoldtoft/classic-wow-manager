Frequently Asked Questions

The longer answer is that it depends. Item prices fluctuate a lot, but generally set items (or tokens for set items) go for the minimum bid most of the time, while rare weapons and trinkets tend to be more expensive.

The best way to get an idea is to check out our Stats page or our Item Log page for a general overview.

The Points System

The short answer is that you earn points by contributing to the raid, and those points are converted into gold.

The longer answer is that every player starts with 100 base points. You can then earn additional points by having a key role, such as being a tank, placing high on overall damage or healing, and through a series of contributing actions like applying sunders, decursing, using engineering, and more. For a full list of rewards and deductions, please see our Rules.

Once the raid is over, there will be a total number of points earned across all players and a total amount of gold collected. For simplicity, let’s say there is 100,000 gold in the shared gold pot and 5,000 total points. You divide 100,000 gold by 5,000 points, giving the value of one point (in this example, 1 point = 20 gold). So if you personally earned 140 points for your contribution, your share of the gold would be 140 × 20 = 2,800 gold.

In short: (Total gold ÷ Total points) × Your points = Your gold

If you want to see real examples, visit any completed raid from the Completed Raids list on our front page and check the Raidlogs and Gold pages for that event.

Why the Name?

Short answer: The name comes from the idea of First Principles.

Longer answer: A long time ago, I wanted the name First Principles, but it was already taken. So instead, I used 1Principles (which was also shorter). Over time, this naturally became One Principles or simply 1P for short.

But what does First Principles mean?
First principles are the most basic, foundational truths or assumptions that cannot be deduced from anything else. In problem-solving and reasoning, it means breaking a concept, system, or problem down into its simplest components and reasoning upward from there, rather than relying on analogy, precedent, or convention.

This approach is common in physics, philosophy, and business strategy, and is often associated with rigorous innovation and clearer decision-making.

Why Discord Sign-In?

Short answer: Your Discord User ID is linked to your characters, and yes, it’s safe.

Longer answer: By using your Discord User ID, we can connect you to your characters, raids, items, assignments, and more. This allows us to show you only your personal raid assignments and highlight logs that belong to you.

Is it safe?
Yes, it’s completely safe. When you log in with Discord, you will see exactly what permissions we request. We only ask for the minimum necessary, such as knowing which servers you are in, so we can verify that you are on our server. We have even opted out of requesting your email address, since many people value privacy and may have personal details in their email.

Do You Hard Reserve or Pre-Sell?

Short answer: No.

Longer answer: In the past, we have pre-sold certain items, such as bindings, but we no longer do this. Experience has shown that items like bindings can sell for much higher amounts in open auctions (up to 250,000 gold), making pre-selling at a lower fixed price unfair to the raid as a whole.

Any exceptions?
Yes. If you are after a specific item and absolutely must be the first to obtain it, you may reach out to us, and we will see if an arrangement can be made. However, it must benefit the entire raid. We will never make secret back-door deals, so there is no need to ask about those.
