Rules

Automatic Rewards & Deductions

Damage Rankings
Top DPS and tanks receive points based on placement. Higher ranks earn more points, descending by position as configured.
Healer Rankings
Top healers receive points based on placement. Higher ranks earn more points, descending by position as configured.
God Gamer (DPS)
+30 pts if #1 DPS exceeds #2 by ≥250,000 damage; +20 pts if by ≥150,000.
God Gamer (Healer)
+20 pts if #1 healing exceeds #2 by ≥250,000; +15 pts if by ≥150,000.
Engineering & Holywater
Points = (abilities used × avg targets) ÷ 10, up to +20 pts.
Major Mana Potions
+1 pts per 3 potions used above 10 used, up to +10 pts.
Dark or Demonic Runes
+1 pt per 2 runes used. Max 15 points.
Interrupted Spells
+1 pt per interrupt, up to +5 pts.
Disarmed Enemies
+1 pt per disarm, up to +5 pts.
Sunder Armor
Points by total sunders: <50: -10 pts, 50–99: 0 pts, 100–119: +5 pts, 120+: +10 pts.
Curse of Recklessness
>70% uptime grants +10 pts.
Curse of Shadow
>70% uptime grants +10 pts.
Curse of the Elements
>70% uptime grants +10 pts.
Faerie Fire
>70% uptime grants +10 pts.
Scorch
0–99: 0 pts, 100–199: +5 pts, ≥200: +10 pts.
Demoralizing Shout
0–99: 0 pts, 100–199: +5 pts, ≥200: +10 pts.
Polymorph
+1 pt per 2 polymorphs, up to +5 pts.
Power Infusion
+1 pt per 2 infusions (excludes self-casts), up to +10 pts.
Decurses
Average-based: +1 pt per 3 above average, penalties below average, from -10 up to +10 pts.
Avoidable Void Damage
Taking avoidable damage applies penalties (e.g., Void Blast: -10 pts, Void Zone: -5 pts).
Frost Resistance
DPS-only: insufficient frost resistance yields penalties (physical: -5 <130, -10 <80; caster: -5 <150, -10 <80).
World Buffs (Missing)
-10 pts per missing required buff below the event’s threshold (e.g., below 4–6 buffs). DMF inclusion may vary by raid.
Big Buyer Bonus
Top 3 spenders (≥25,000 gold) earn: 25k+: +5, 50k+: +10, 75k+: +15, 100k+: +20 pts. One reward per player.
