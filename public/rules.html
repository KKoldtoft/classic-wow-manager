Rules

Manual Rewards & Deductions

Tanks
Main tank, OT1, OT2, OT3, The Four Horsemen tanks, Razuvius tanks, and Twins tanks get bonus points for tanking.
Puller
Hunter puller gets bonus points for pulling.
Summoner
Summoners get bonus points for summoning and clicking.
Kiting
Gluth kiter gets bonus points for kiting Gluth.
ID Fishing
AQ40 and BWL fishers get bonus points for clean AQ40 and Naxx IDs based on how clean they are.
Late Fee
Everyone gets deductions for being late. You must be in the raid and ready to pull no later than 10 minutes before raid start.
No-Show Deductions
If you no-show (including cancellations less than 24 hours in advance), there will be a deduction waiting for you in your next raid. The size of this will depend on circumstances.
Non-Responsive
Not being responsive and not replying to the raid leader will result in a deduction.
General Mistakes
Actions like ninja pulling, taking aggro, and other general mistakes will result in a deduction of points.
Bidding
All bidding is final. Honest mistakes that are caught right away will be respected and wrong bids canceled, but if you bid on an item and later will not or cannot pay for it, you should expect dire consequences.

Automatic Rewards & Deductions

Base Points
Every player participating in the raid automatically receives +100 base points.
Damage Rankings
Top DPS and tanks receive points: 1st=80, 2nd=70, 3rd=55, 4th=40, 5th=35, 6th=30, 7th=25, 8th=20, 9th=15, 10th=10, 11th=8, 12th=6, 13th=5, 14th=4, 15th=3 pts.
Healer Rankings
Top healers receive points: 1st=80, 2nd=65, 3rd=60, 4th=55, 5th=40, 6th=35, 7th=30, 8th=20, 9th=15, 10th=10 pts.
God Gamer (DPS)
+30 pts if #1 DPS exceeds #2 by ≥250,000 damage; +20 pts if by ≥150,000.
God Gamer (Healer)
+20 pts if #1 healing exceeds #2 by ≥250,000; +15 pts if by ≥150,000.
Engineering & Holywater
Points = floor((abilities used × avg targets) ÷ 10), up to +20 pts.
Major Mana Potions
First 10 potions earn 0 pts. After 10, earn +1 pt per 3 additional potions: floor((potions - 10) ÷ 3), up to +10 pts max.
Dark or Demonic Runes
+1 pt per 2 runes used. Max 15 points.
Interrupted Spells
+1 pt per 2 interrupts, up to +5 pts.
Disarmed Enemies
+1 pt per 3 disarms, up to +5 pts.
Sunder Armor
Points vs raid average effective sunders: <25% avg: -20; 25–49%: -15; 50–74%: -10; 75–89%: -5; 90–109%: 0; 110–124%: +5; ≥125%: +10.
Curse of Recklessness
>85% uptime grants +10 pts.
Curse of Shadow
>85% uptime grants +10 pts.
Curse of the Elements
>85% uptime grants +10 pts.
Faerie Fire
>85% uptime grants +10 pts.
Scorch
0–99: 0 pts, 100–199: +5 pts, ≥200: +10 pts.
Demoralizing Shout
0–99: 0 pts, 100–199: +5 pts, ≥200: +10 pts.
Polymorph
+1 pt per 2 polymorphs, up to +5 pts.
Power Infusion
+1 pt per 2 infusions (excludes self-casts), up to +10 pts.
Decurses
Average-based: +1 pt per 3 above average, penalties below average, from -10 up to +10 pts.
Avoidable Void Damage
Taking avoidable damage applies penalties (e.g., Void Blast: -10 pts, Void Zone: -5 pts).
Frost Resistance
DPS-only: insufficient frost resistance yields penalties (physical: -5 <130, -10 <80; caster: -5 <150, -10 <80).
World Buffs (Missing)
Required buffs: 4 if not Naxx; 6 if Naxx. -10 pts per missing buff. DMF counts only if ≥10 players have it.
Big Buyer Bonus
Top 3 spenders (≥25,000 gold) can earn: 25k:+5, 50k:+10, 75k:+15, 100k:+20 pts (when configured). One reward per player.
Totems (Shaman)
Windfury: points vs group baseline of extra attacks (tank group has 75% requirement): <75% baseline: 0; 75–99%: +10; 100–125%: +15; >125%: +20. Grace/Strength: if group avg ≥75% baseline and ≥10 totems: +1 per 10 totems (Grace max +20; Strength max +10). Tranquil Air: +1 per 10 totems, max +5.
Shaman Healers
Top 3 Shaman healers earn: 25 / 20 / 15 pts.
Priest Healers
Top 2 Priest healers earn: 20 / 15 pts.
Druid Healers
Top Druid healer earns: 15 pts.
Attendance Streaks
Consecutive attendance awards: 4=+3, 5=+6, 6=+9, 7=+12, 8+=+15 pts.
Guild Members
Confirmed guild members present earn a flat +10 pts.
Weekly Attendance Bonus
Players attending multiple NAX raids in the same week (Wednesday–Tuesday) earn bonus points: 2nd raid: +5 pts, 3rd+ raid: +10 pts.
Confirmed Assignments
Players who confirm all their raid assignments before the raid start earn +10 pts. View and confirm assignments on the Assignments page.
Too Low Damage
DPS penalties vs active fight time: <150 DPS: -100; 150–199.9: -50; 200–249.9: -25.
Too Low Healing
Healer penalties vs active fight time: <85 HPS: -100; 85–99.9: -50; 100–124.9: -25.
Goblin Rocket Helmet
+5 pts if wearing a Goblin Rocket Helmet during the raid.
